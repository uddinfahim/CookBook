Meonese

Ingredient Quantity
Egg Two Pcs
Oil One Cup
Suger One tea spoon
Salt One pinch
Black Paper 1/2 tea spoon

Preparation

First time we have to put egg in blinder then blend it for five second. Then step by step should put oil in the blend for five second in every step. when it will gravy then sholud mixed salt, suger and black paper again should blend for five second. Now it ready to serve.
