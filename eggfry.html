Egg Fry

Ingredient Quantity
Egg One Pcs
Onion One Pcs
Green Chili Two Pcs
Oil Queter cup
Salt one pinch

Preparation

First of all we have to slice the onion then slice chili. After getting onion and chili slice then we should mixed some salt. Then we have smear all those things. Then egg will be mixed also.
On the other side we are taking fry pen on the stove, wait for two or three miniutes, when take some hit the egg mixer should pour on the pen. Now stove hit should low. In low hit egg cooked very well. Now ready our egg fry.
